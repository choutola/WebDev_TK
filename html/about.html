About us

Titya Travel & Tours Co.,Ltd was established on April 1994, our company has grown strong and now it's one of the leading tour operators in South East Asian Region, and we literally connect travelers from Asia, Europe, United State, Australia and other parts of the world. Our philosophy is to give online best services, Convenient and Reliable to our travel partners.PTM Travel & Tours is now located in Phnom Penh, Cambodia as its head office and having two branches in Phnom Penh, one in Siem Reap. Our various GSA/Sales & Marketing offices are located in the countries like Singapore, Malaysia, India, Hong Kong, China, Philippines, Indonesia, Laos, Myanmar and Vietnam. And we also have our marketing office in European countries, Austria, Germany, Switzerland, UK, United State, Ireland, Australia and in the Middle East. We deliver our products innovation and service oriented to our business partners and customers. PTM Travel & Tours, our staffs are very well trained to assist you and base on their experiences to provide good value added services to you at any time. We have our own pride in the modern world of fleet is composed in our 15 coaches, which are fully air-con mini vans, buses & cars. PTM Travel & Tours is an end-to-end solution that hosts, manages, and distributes your travel and leisure content across the Asia-Pacific and Worldwide with Automates inventory management process.

As an International Air Transportation Association (IATA) appointed agent

- Worldwide Travel Network through PTM Travel & Tours System
- Service backup by a team of professional trained ticketing / tours consultants
- Visa and Travel document services
- Airport transfers arrangement

- Automates inventory management process.

Titya Travel & Tours Network is the first fully integrated solution for travel and leisure suppliers in the Asia-Pacific and Worldwide. Travel and leisure suppliers of content such as tour packages, cruises, ferries, rails, land transport, insurance will now be able to reap the benefits of content hosting, management and electronic distribution. Your reach will be extended and your distribution costs slashed.The result? A significant increase in your revenue and a reduction in your costs leading to more profit for you.PTM Travel & Tours Network employs state-of-the-art technology to automate travel content hosting, management and distribution. The different modules of the system work off a single database, thereby enhancing operational efficiencies.
